São Paulo's Subway is at the epicenter of urban transportation modernization in Brazil, with record investments and expansion of lines that promise to transform urban mobility and boost the economic development of the city and the country.

<p>"Speed is also a form of civilization", a sentence that deeply resonates at the heart of the São Paulo Subway expansion and modernization project. With a record budget of R$ 5.4 billion for 2026, the city is not only moving away from antiquity but also establishing a bridge to a more integrated and efficient future in terms of urban mobility.</p>
<p>The core of the expansion plan is Line 2-Green, which foresees 13.8 km of new tracks and 13 new stations<a href="https://exame.com/brasil/expansao-das-linhas-2-15-e-entrega-da-17-plano-de-r-54-bi-do-metro-de-sp/" target="_blank">1</a>. This extension is not just a technical advance, but a response to the growing mobility demands of the population, linking significant areas and stimulating economic development along its path.</p>
<p>Line 15-Silver, one of the monorails in commercial operation in Brazil, will receive R$ 1.03 billion to expand its reach in São Paulo's eastern zone<a href="https://exame.com/brasil/expansao-das-linhas-2-15-e-entrega-da-17-plano-de-r-54-bi-do-metro-de-sp/" target="_blank">2</a>. This initiative strengthens transportation capacity and connectivity between different regions of the city, promoting a better distribution of people flow and economic activities.</p>
<p>Line 17-Gold has a budget of R$ 836.3 million to complete the section between Congonhas Airport and Morumbi Station<a href="https://exame.com/brasil/expansao-das-linhas-2-15-e-entrega-da-17-plano-de-r-54-bi-do-metro-de-sp/" target="_blank">3</a>. This segment is symbolic, as its completion has been promised since the 2014 World Cup, and its finalization will bring significant relief to the city's mobility system.</p>
<p>The investment in CBTC (Communication-Based Train Control) technology is a central piece in this expansion<a href="https://exame.com/brasil/expansao-das-linhas-2-15-e-entrega-da-17-plano-de-r-54-bi-do-metro-de-sp/" target="_blank">4</a>. CBTC increases operational efficiency, allowing trains to operate with shorter intervals and greater safety, resulting in more trains per hour and, consequently, greater transportation capacity.</p>
<p>The acquisition of 63 new trains for various lines of the Subway<a href="https://exame.com/brasil/expansao-das-linhas-2-15-e-entrega-da-17-plano-de-r-54-bi-do-metro-de-sp/" target="_blank">5</a> is an unequivocal sign of the renewal of rolling stock, essential for increasing transportation capacity and reducing maintenance costs, while also increasing the reliability of the system.</p>
<p>This cycle of expansion and modernization of São Paulo's Subway reflects the need for an update in Brazilian urban transportation<a href="https://www.ocafezinho.com/2026/06/08/expansao-do-metro-de-sao-paulo-reflete-necessidade-de-modernizacao-no-transporte-urbano-brasileiro/" target="_blank">6</a>. The modernization of the subway system not only meets the immediate demands of urban mobility but also contributes to sustainability and the city's competitiveness in the global scenario.</p>
<p>Connecting regions, promoting economic development, and improving the quality of life of Paulistanos, São Paulo's Subway is writing a new chapter in the country's development, where technology and infrastructure join forces to build a more integrated and efficient urban mobility reality.</p>
