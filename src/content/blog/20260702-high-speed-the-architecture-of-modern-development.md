The advancement of high-speed railways reflects the march of civilization, organizing urban mobility and driving economic development.

<p>"Speed is also a form of civilization", says a proverb that highlights the essence of high-speed transportation. In this dizzying universe, tracks are not just a mode of transit; they symbolize modernity in motion, a link between cities, a fine sand of the productivity clock, and a driver of regional development.</p>
<p>Although Brazil is known worldwide for its extensive highways and a transportation infrastructure that still challenges the imagination today, the country finds itself at the epicenter of a mobility paradigm that demands a new strategic approach. Investment in high-speed railways and metros is no longer an option; it becomes an imperative for sustainable development and the economic integration of the country.</p>
<p>As <a href="https://aecom.com/markets/transportation/high-speed-rail/" target="_blank">points out the Aecom report</a>, high-speed infrastructure involves much more than track construction. It is the integration of a network that encompasses strategy, planning, design, engineering, program management, and digital delivery, aiming to optimize performance and value in the long term.</p>
<p>The impact is multifaceted, from reducing travel time, increasing transportation capacity, improving international connectivity to promoting sustainable mobility. The <a href="https://www.eesi.org/papers/view/fact-sheet-high-speed-rail-development-worldwide" target="_blank">EESI</a> highlights that countries like China and Europe have already recognized the potential of these projects over the last decades, and Brazil should not be an exception.</p>
<p>High speed not only opens new horizons for urban mobility but also reconfigures the economic landscape, allowing us to travel faster and more comfortably. The <a href="https://mediarail.wordpress.com/high-speed-trains/" target="_blank">Mediarail</a> reports success cases around the globe, where liberalization and innovation have transformed the daily routine.</p>
<p>The success factors for high-speed projects, according to research conducted by the <a href="https://uil.be.uw.edu/2023/06/01/new-research-led-by-uil-identifies-success-factors-for-high-speed-rail-projects/" target="_blank">UIL</a>, include a service-focused approach and providing value in the transportation market, the need to avoid political renegotiation, and the importance of productive relationships with the private sector.</p>
<p>High speed is a symbol of modernity, a catalyst for development, and a tool for regional integration. For Brazil, it is crucial to recognize this reality and align infrastructure strategies so that speed becomes a vehicle for national progress.</p>
